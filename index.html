Questions & Answers

What’s the dress code?

Cocktail attire for the rehearsal dinner, formal (black time optional) attire.

How do I get to the venue? Is there parking?

Transportation to the wedding venue will be provided. For those taking a taxi, there is onsite parking.

What will the weather be like?

Spring in Verona is usually mild, with cooler evenings. A light jacket or wrap is recommended for outdoor moments between events. ceremony and cocktail outside. reception and after party inside.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. Please share any dietary restrictions or allergies on your RSVP, and we’ll coordinate with the venue and caterer.

When is the best time to book flights?

From our research, book flights by Jan 26. fly out on a tuesday or wednesday. Verona does have a sizeable airport.
